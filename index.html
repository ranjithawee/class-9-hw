Boolean Atribute

A boolean attribute is used to store the true or false value of a condition. For example, a boolean can store whether or not a visitor had added

items to their cart or visited a specific section of site. The boolean attribute is available in the following scopes: Event, Visit, Visitor.

boolean atributes are used in 3 diffirent ways, Examples are given bellow:
